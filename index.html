Welcome to the Genesis Hub. This site maintains the vision, culture, and community for the Genesis multicreator gaming universe. So, if this is your first time here, check out the Vision section to learn more about Genesis.

Vision

Genesis is a multicreator gaming universe, with a diverse community working together to achieve a common goal: A metaverse that will still be growing, evolving, and actively played in 100 years.

To achieve this, development and management of Genesis will be decentralized, with governance decisions guided by the Genesis Foundation, and voted on by players using the GENESIS token. Genesis will run as a nonprofit, with all assets owned by and revenue earned by the Genesis Foundation reinvested in the long-term success of the game.

The Vision section contains an overview of Genesis, including its culture, community, governance, tokenomics, and roadmap.

Community & Governance

Genesis is community-developed and community-goverened. The Governance section lays out the Genesis governance processes and procedures, and the Community Improvement Proposals (CIPs) section contains all current and past community proposals.

You can also find important documents such as community guidelines and terms of service in the Resources section.

Improvements

Geneis is, and always will be, a work in progress. You can make change happen. Find a typo in the site? Open a pull request on the Genesis Github. Or, if you have a suggestion you want to implement, head over to the Community Improvement Proposals, and start to make your mark on the future of Genesis.
